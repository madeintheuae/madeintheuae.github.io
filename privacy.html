Privacy Policy

This policy explains how MENAFactory collects, uses, and protects personal information when you use our website and services.

What We Collect

  • Account details: name, email, role, company.
  • Usage data: IP, device, browser, pages visited, language.
  • Content you submit: campaigns, uploads, messages.
  • Payment data processed via third-party providers.

How We Use Data

  • Provide and improve the platform and support.
  • Process transactions and manage subscriptions.
  • Send service updates, security notices, and (optionally) marketing.
  • Protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats.

Legal Bases (where applicable)

Consent, contract performance, legitimate interests, and legal obligations.

Sharing

We share data with service providers (hosting, payments, analytics) under confidentiality terms. We may share if required by law or to protect rights and safety. We do not sell personal data.

Retention

We keep data while your account is active and as needed for legal/compliance needs. Backups may persist for a limited period.

Your Choices

  • Access, correct, or delete your data (where legally permitted).
  • Opt out of marketing communications.
  • Disable cookies in your browser (some features may be limited).

Cookies & Tracking

We use necessary and functional cookies; analytics and advertising cookies may be used to improve experience. Manage cookies via your browser settings.

Security

We use technical and organizational measures (encryption in transit, access controls, logging). No system is 100% secure; use strong passwords and protect your account.

Children

The service is not directed to children under 18. Do not submit data for minors.

International Transfers

Data may be processed in other countries with protections aligned to this policy. We use safeguards when required.

Updates

We may update this policy; the latest version is posted here.

Contact

Email privacy@menafactory.com for privacy questions or requests.
