Acceptable Use Policy

MITU must be used lawfully and respectfully. Prohibited uses include:

  • Illegal activity or solicitation of unlawful acts.
  • Harassment, discrimination, or threats.
  • IP infringement, impersonation, or fraudulent claims.
  • Malware, scraping, or attempts to bypass security.
  • Publishing sensitive personal data without consent.
  • Hosting or promoting violence, exploitation, or adult content.
  • Spam, bulk unsolicited messages, or denial-of-service attacks.
  • Excessive resource consumption that degrades service for others.

Enforcement

We may suspend or terminate accounts, remove content, or contact authorities for violations. Repeat or severe violations may lead to permanent account closure.

Security

Maintain your account credentials securely. Do not probe, scan, or attack the platform.

Reporting

Report abuse via contact. We review and act where appropriate.
